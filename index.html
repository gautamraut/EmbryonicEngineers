Why Work With Us??

Embroynic Engineers India deploys the globally proven STEM focus approach to help kids gain the skills required to succeed in today's challenging world. This includes the ability to think critically, solve complex problems, and drive advancements in science and technology. STEM enables the child to understand and learn the concepts by performing them practically and gain hands-on experience with the help of the already available technology and digital tools. It is an integrative and inter-disciplinary way of learning.

STEM education includes every field under Science, Technology, engineering and Math right from chemistry to Physics, Software Design to Trigonometry. Moreover, it also helps children to gain skills like Collaborative; project based learning which is imperative to have a good career in 21st century.

Our Mission

Every child has a natural creativity and energy which we believe needs a right channel and direction to realize its true potential. At Early Engineers, our mission is to empower our children through providing this much needed channel by providing a platform which helps them explore their natural creativity in a fun-filled way.

Our Mission is to provide an enriching professional skill-based education to students, supporting and enhancing school-curriculum, helping them develop their confidence, ability to ask questions, team-work and leadership skills and give an enabling environment for them to be successful.

What all we offer??

Appropriate structured courses are offered for children in primary through senior school and all the way up to college students. Each session comprises of distinct projects with specific learning outcomes in concrete way.

Age Group : 5 – 9 years

Software Engineering

Understanding of concept with hands-on session following code.org curriculum. Students will learn about binary system, computational thinking, algorithm, loop, conditional thinking, functions, bugs and debugging in a fun and interactive way while working on Scratch/Blockly.

Robotics

Students will be understanding various science concepts using age appropriate Educational kits. Students learns concepts with initial start on early robotics. They learn about concepts with using motor, sensors, gears with various models while working on robotics kits.

Aerospace Engineering

Understanding of science behind various flying machines and model creation of rockets, aeroplanes, hot air balloons, parachutes etc.



Age Group : 9 – 14 years

Software Engineering

Understanding of concept with hands-on session following code.org curriculum. Students will learn about binary system, computational thinking, algorithm, loop, conditional thinking, functions, bugs and debugging in a fun and interactive way while working on Scratch/Blockly.

Mobile App Designing

​Students will learn the basic concepts behind mobile application development by starting with a simple game creation along with physical activities, which makes learning both fun and educational. Students will take back the developed Android application and course material at the end of the class.
